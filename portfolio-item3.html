Android Weather App

(Java)

The app fetches the current location of the user and performs geocoding to get the city name & country.

Using the obtained latitude and longitude from the location, the app fetches the weather information from the API. Every time the location updates, the weather information also updates accordingly and reflects on screen for the user. To fetch the weather information, the app used https://www.weatherapi.com/.
